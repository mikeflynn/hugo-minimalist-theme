About The Ugly Donkey.com

This site is written and poorly edited by me, Mike Flynn (@thatmikeflynn). I previously wrote the successful Punching Kitty project that was focused on news and events for St. Louis, MO, winning several awards along the way.

The Ugly Donkey was started in 2016, shortly after the election of Donald Trump. Why? Because I had been looking for a new writing project and figured that national politics was the best place for a laugh and some critical thinking at the moment.

Also, if that Gen. Mike Flynn gets a cabinet seat I gotta keep my Google juice flowing somehow.
